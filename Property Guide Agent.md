## An AI property tour guide deployed at a live open house.

60+ guests. A noisy, crowded environment. The AI handled every conversation with **99% accuracy**. Guests didn't just use it — they enjoyed it.

---

## The Problem

Property viewings depend entirely on agent availability. When an agent is unavailable, buyers wait — or they leave. Every missed showing is a potential deal lost.

The system allowed the property to remain open for self-guided tours without requiring a full-time agent on site.

---

## The Product Experience

A buyer receives a temporary access code. When they unlock the front door:

- AI calls them within 60 seconds
- Greets them by name
- Guides them through the property room by room — explaining features, answering questions, and prompting them toward the next space
- Answers questions in real time
- Keeps the conversation warm — small talk included

The focus was never just information delivery. It was making people feel genuinely cared for and respected.

---

## System Architecture

**Door unlock → Webhook → AI call → Guided property tour**

When the smart lock is triggered, a webhook sends the buyer's details. Within 60 seconds the system initiates a call and routes the conversation to one of two conversational personas trained on the full property knowledge base. Both personas use carefully designed conversational voices to make interactions feel warm and natural.

A QR code trigger was added for the open house event to allow guests to initiate a call manually.
